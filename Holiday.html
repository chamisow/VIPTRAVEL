SRI LANKA TOURS

DAY 01 (JULY): AIRPORT – NEGOMBO 

20 KMS

When arrived at the Katunayake international airport at 05:00 hrs. Welcome by a representative of SCENIC VENTURES; and transfer to NEGOMBO to check in to JETWING BLUE HOTEL for rest and leisure. (Deluxe – BB)



MORE

DAY 02 (JULY): NEGOMBO – DAMBULLA

180 KMS

After breakfast transfer to DAMBULLA via PINNAWELA ELEPHANT ORPHANAGE. And travel to DAMBULLA to can visit CEVE TEMPLE COMPLEX, GOLDEN TEMPLE, and SPICE GARDENS etc. check in to THILANKA RESORTS & SPA (Luxury – HB)

MORE

DAY 03 & 04 (JULY): DAMBULLA – TRINCOMALEE 140 KMS  

140 KMS

After breakfast check out from the hotel for a day of excitement in the MINNERIYA NATIONAL PARK WILD LIFE SAFARI, after this travel to climb the famous SIGIRIYA ROCK FORTRESS. And then travel to TRINCOMALEE for overnight stay at CHAAYA BLU. (Superior – HB)

MORE

DAY 05 (JULY): TRINCOMALEE – KANDY

220 KMS

After breakfast check out from the hotel and travel to KANDY via POLONNARUWA and visit BOTANICAL GARDEN and other key sites in KANDY. At 17:30hrs witness the KANDYAN DANCE. Also visit the “TEMPLE OF TOOTH RELIC” 19:00hrs. Watch the traditional rituals performed daily by the religious dignitaries of the temple in keeping with the cultural heritage of the country. Lastly you will visit the “RAJA WASALA”, check-in at AMAARA SKY HOTEL (Superior – HB)

MORE

DAY 06 (JULY): KANDY – NUWARA ELIYA

120 KMS

After breakfast leave for travel to NUWARA ELIYA, visit HORTON PLAINS, THE WORLD’S END, LAKE GRAGORY, TEA ESTATES, and TEA MUSEUM and check in to COCOON HILLS BOUTIQUE for overnight stay (Superior – HB)

MORE

DAY 07 (JULY): NUWARA ELIYA – COLOMBO – AIRPORT        

250 KMS

After breakfast check out from the hotel and travel via KITULGALA to go for WILD WHITE WATER RAFTING at KELINI RIVER where the famous movie “BRIDGE ON THE RIVER KWAI” was filmed. Then to COLOMBO CITY TOUR, you can visit BMICH, BEIRA LAKE & TEMPLE, OLD PARLIAMENT, GALLE FACE GREEN, PETTAH MARKET, INDEPENDENCE SQUARE and last minutes shop since this is the last day.

MORE

                                Transfer to the airport to connect to your flight. (Reporting time at airport 3 hours before flight as required by airline)
