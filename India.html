GOLDEN TRIANGLE

D01 ARRIVE DELHI (No Meals)
Met, and transfer to your hotel for overnight. (Hotel check-in/out times
are 12 Noon).

D02 DELHI – AGRA (B) – 4/5 hours
Morning drive to Agra. Visit world famous TAJ MAHAL (Friday Closed),
built by Mughal Emperor Shahjehan in memory of his queen Mumtaz Mahal to
enshrine her mortal remains. Later visit massive Agra Fort. Overnight.

D03 AGRA – JAIPUR (B) – 5.5 hours
Morning drive to Jaipur, known as Pink City. Check-in at your hotel.
Evening visit beautiful Birla Temple with your driver. Overnight at your
hotel.

D04 JAIPUR (B)
Morning drive to Amber Fort, enroute photo stop at most famous landmark
of Jaipur, Hawa Mahal (Palace of Winds), continue to Amber Fort, once
the ancient capital of Rajput Empire, see the Sheesh Mahal ,
Dewan-e-Khas, Dewan-e-Aam inside the Fort. Later visit City Palace &
Museum, Observatory built in 18th century by Jai Singh, a famous
astronomer, and also drive through the Business and residential areas of
Pink City.

D05 JAIPUR-DELHI (5.5 hours) – DEPARTURE (B)
Early morning drive to Delhi. Enjoy tour of Old & New Delhi, Qutab Tower
(12 century AD) one of the most perfect towers in the world, drive past
India Gate (war memorial) & Embassy area (Diplomatic Enclave), Govt.
buildings (1921 – 30 AD), President House & Parliament House. Drive past
Old Delhi Areas & also drive past Red Fort & Jamma Mosque. Later in
time, transfer to airport for your onward flight.

KERELA

Kerala, India’s most interesting southern state where the culture has
been influenced by Portuguese, Dutch and English elements involved in
the spice trade, its geography varies in a very short distance from
tropical beaches to high hills. We present you below the Must See
highlights of Kerala like Houseboat Cruise, Unique Backwaters & Resorts,
Age Old Ayurvedic Spa and cultural city of Kochi (Cochin).

5 Days Must See of Kerala

D01 ARRIVE COCHIN
Met and transfer to your hotel for an overnight.

D02 COCHIN – ALLEPPEY (B/L/D) > 2 hours

Drive to Alleppey to board your private Houseboat for a cruise on the
Backwaters. The complex network of lagoons, lakes, rivers and canals
fringing the coast of Kerala forms the basis of a distinct regional
lifestyle. Enjoy a cruise on the emerald palm fringed backwaters of
Kerala. Lunch, Dinner & Overnight on board your Houseboat.

D03 ALLEPPEY – KUMARAKOM (B)

Morning enjoy another short cruise and after Breakfast, your houseboat
drops you at Kumarakom. Check-into a Backwaters Resort. You may try the
famous Ayurvedic Spa/Massage at your resort at own costs. Relax at your
Resort located in the beautiful serene backwaters on banks of famous
Vembanand lake with picturesque surroundings.

D04 KUMARAKOM – COCHIN (B) > 2 hours

Morning drive to Cochin. Visit St. Francis Church, the oldest European
built church in India, constructed in 1503 by Portugues San Franciscan
friars. It was renamed St. Francis in 1663. In 1779 the Protestant Dutch
replaced the original wooden building with stone masonry. Later walk
around the Jew Street and visit Jewish Synagogue (Closed on Friday, Sat
& Jewish Holidays), built in 1568 AD, and is the oldest in the
common-wealth. It was destroyed by the Portuguese in 1662, but rebuilt
by the Dutch 2 years later. It is adorned by hundreds of blue and white
hand-painted tiles. Also visit 1555 Portuguese built Mattancherry Palace
which was gifted to the Maharaja of Kochi as a good-will gesture to
ensure trade privileges. The Palace is now a Museum (Closed on Fri &
Sat). Continue onto the giant Chinese Fishing nets at the harbor’s
entrance that silhouetted against sunset, present a magnificent sight of
the water-front.

D05 COCHIN-DEP (B)

Today you may try some good shopping at Cochin’s MG.Road. (Check-out at
12 Noon). Later transfer to airport for your onward flight.

HIMALAYAN PANORAMA

8 Days Himalayan Panorama (Shimla/Manali)

01 Arrive-Delhi (No Meals)
Met on arrival and transfer to your hotel. (Check-in time 12 Noon).
Overnight.

Day 02 Delhi – Shimla (B)
After breakfast drive 8/9 hours to Shimla, Check in at your hotel.
Overnight.

Day 03 Shimla (B)

This morning proceed to visit Kufri- 16 Kms away from Shimla, which
provides ski slopes & is the centre for winter sports in the state. Here
one can hire a pony on direct payment to go up to the certain hill top
from where one can enjoy the view of Himalayan range. Later stroll
around the Mall shopping area and towards Ridge where enjoy the nature.
Overnight.

Day 04 Shimla / Manali (B)
Drive 9 hours to Manali through beautiful Mountain Roads with fantastic
scenery, Check in at your hotel.

Day 05 Manali – Rohtang Pass – Manali (B)
Today excursion to Rohtang Pass, (Upto Snow Point as per weather
conditions), gateway of ancient trading routes to Lahaul, Ladakh &
Central Asia; and a divide between the lush beauty of Kullu Valley and
the stark hauntingly beautiful landscape of Lahaul. The pass is closed
during winter months till snows melt in summers, enroute pass through
the picturesque village of Kothi, which offers views of glaciers and
deep gorges through which the Beas River flows. Return back to Manali &
take a stroll at The Mall Road.

Day 06 Manali (B)
Today visit 450 year old Dhoongri temple dedicated to goddess Hadimba.
Manali has a Tibetan monastery which as several images of the Buddha,
thanka paintings and a handicrafts center. Nearby are the hot sulphur
springs at Vaishist perched on a steep hill-side below the Temple. Water
has been piped to shower fitted bath-rooms. Visit local market where you
may pick-up famous Kullu shawls and colorful caps.

Day 07 Manali-Chandigarh – Delhi (B) Drive 8 hours + Train Travel
Morning drive to Chandigarh to connect Shatabadi Express Train to New
Delhi, Leave Chandigarh at 1820 hrs. Arrive Delhi 2140 hrs. Met and
transfer to your hotel. Overnight.
Day 08 Delhi – Departure (B) (Hotel Check-Out time 12 Noon).
Time free till transfer to airport for your onward flight.

BUDDHIST CIRCUIT

Day One

CMB-GAYA UL 2941 ETA 0900 hrs. (L/D)
Arrive Gaya, Met & transfer to Lotus Nikko. Later visit Mahabodhi
Temple, Bodhi Tree where Lord Buddha was enlightenment, Great Buddha
Statue, Niranjana River. Overnight.

Day Two

BODHGAYA-Rajgir-Nalanda-PATNA (B)
Drive 3 hrs to Rajgir to visit World Peace Stupa by Cable Car. From here
one could see the glimpse of Vulture Peak (where Lord Buddha meditated),
visit Bimbirara Jail, Caves & Veluvanarmaya. Then short drive to Nalanda
to visit ruins of world oldest University, containing a number of
Buddhist Stupas & Chaityas, Monasteries, Hostels etc. Onward drive 3 hrs
to Patna. Overnight at Chanakya Towers.

Day Three

PATNA-Vaishali-Kesaria-KUSHINAGAR (B/L/D) > 6 hrs drive + 3 hrs enroute
visit
Drive to Kushinagar via visiting Vaishali, where Lord Buddhia delivered
his last sermon before going for Paramnirvana. Onwards drive to visit
Kesaria, one of the world’s largest Stupa. Then to Kushinagar. to visit
Mahaparnirvana Temple (Lord Buddha attained Mahaparinirvana) and Rambhar
Stupa (Cremation Site). Overnight at Lotus Nikko.

Day Four

KUSHINAGAR-LUMBINI (B/L/D) > 5 hrs drive
Drive to Lumbini, while going stop at India/Nepal border for Immigration
(Ensure Double Entry India Visa within 60 days Re-Entry Endorsement &
Single Entry for Nepal). Arrive Lumbini & visit birth place of Lord
Buddha, Ashok Pillar, Maya Devi Pond etc. Overnight at Hotel Nansc

Day Five

LUMBINI-Kapilvastu-SRAVASTI (B/L/D) – 6-7 hrs drive
Drive to Sravasti via Kapilvastu where Lord Buddha spent his childhood.
Arr Sravasti. Check-in Lotus Nikko. Later visit Jetvana, Anand Bodhi
Tree, ruins of numerous shrines, Stupas & monasteries.

Day Six

SRAVASTI-VARANASI (B/L/D) > 7-8 hrs drive
Drive to Varanasi. Check-in Hindustan Intl/Grand Meraden. Visit Sarnath,
where Buddha delivered his first sermon after he got enlightenment. Also
visit Sarnath Museum & prayers at Temple.

Day Seven

VARANASI-GAYA (B/L/D) > 5 hrs drive.
Drive to Bodhgaya, Check-in at Lotus Nikko. Re-visit Mahabodhi Temple.
Overnight.

Day Eight

GAYA-CMB (B) > UL 2942 ETD 1030 hrs
Transfer to airport for your flight to CMB
